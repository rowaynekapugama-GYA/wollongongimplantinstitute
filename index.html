A solution that lasts

Missing or damaged teeth change more than your smile

When teeth are missing or damaged, eating, speaking and smiling all become harder — and that takes a toll on your confidence. Unlike a removable denture, an implant replaces the root of the tooth, bonding with your jawbone to create a stable foundation for a fixed, natural-looking replacement.

  • Fixed in place, so you can eat and speak with confidence
  • Natural-looking replacement teeth
  • Helps preserve jawbone and facial structure
  • A long-term alternative to loose dentures
A titanium implant replaces the tooth root for a fixed, lasting result.

Your options

Treatments tailored to you

From a single tooth to a full arch, your treatment is planned around your needs at your free consultation.

Dental implants

For one or several missing teeth. A titanium implant replaces the tooth root and supports a fixed, natural-looking crown or bridge.

  • Single and multiple tooth replacement
  • A fixed alternative to a denture or bridge
  • Designed to look and feel like your own teeth
All-on-4

All-on-4 full arch

A full upper or lower set of fixed teeth supported by just four implants — a streamlined approach to replacing a whole arch.

  • A full arch supported by four implants
  • Often suitable when bone density is reduced
  • A fixed solution, not a removable denture

Good to know

Common questions

Many people with missing or damaged teeth are suitable, including those finding dentures uncomfortable or who have been told conventional implants are difficult due to reduced bone. The only way to know for certain is a consultation, where your suitability is assessed in person.

Your comfort is a priority. We offer local anaesthetic, happy gas and IV sedation (also known as sleep dentistry), and will discuss the right approach for you so the experience is as comfortable as possible.

The investment depends on how many teeth are being replaced and the treatment that suits you, which is confirmed at your consultation. We offer flexible payment plans, can help eligible patients access early release of super, and will explain any private health rebates that may apply.

Eligible patients may be able to access their superannuation early to help fund dental treatment. Eligibility is decided by the relevant authorities, not the practice, so we recommend seeking independent advice. We are happy to guide you through how the process generally works.

All-on-4 is designed to be a streamlined way to replace a full arch with four implants. Timeframes vary between patients, and your personalised plan will set out what to expect from start to finish.
